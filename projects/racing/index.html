Racing

I was a member of Columbia University Formula Racing from 2019 to 2021, and did some cool projects at the intersection of computer science, electrical engineering, and mechanical engineering. On the team, I was the lead for the wiring harness of our Internal Combustion Engine racecar.

Wiring harness schematic

After a month of hard work, I produced a full schematic of our previously undocumented wiring harness with the help of other members. It was not completely polished, and, with the team's constant prototyping, a full schematic likely never will be! We had recently wired up a pneumatic paddle shifter setup, and I had yet to document the components and dozens of wires.

I used KiCad to make this schematic. All the files for this schematic are in this directory.
