Below are links to subscribable calendars for various open recreational sports offered at UC Berkeley. Possible use case: add the link to your google calendar, and see on your calendar when you can play the sports.

Lap swim Pickleball Badminton Basketball Volleyball Climbing General RSF hours

Technical details

These links point to iCal files (.ics) which are updated every hour. They reflect data which is on UCB Recreation and Wellbeing pages (e.g. the one for lap swim). All of these pages (except the general RSF one) ultimately draw data from this internal page, which is what I scrape to update the .ics files. The code is available in this website's repository.
