This page shows the availability of tennis courts in Berkeley, CA today. It is designed to help find a non-reserved court to go play at for free. My server tries to update it every hour.

The data are pulled from court reservation information on the city's recreation website with the following rules:

  • Any available reservation times are marked as available.
  • Other times within the reservation period of the court are marked as unavailable, as it is presumably reserved. (note: I don't know the reservation period of most of the courts)
  • Any other times are marked as available
The 1hr blocks I show extend 06:00-22:00 as that is when the parks are officially open.
